    About TNC

    The News Channel (TNC) is a multilingual, transnational community of writers, translators, academics, and human rights advocates that is run entirely by volunteers. We use the internet's potential to help people understand each other across borders. How are we going to accomplish this?

    • Reporting: Our bilingual journalistic staff covers stories about people whose perspectives and experiences are rarely heard in the mainstream media.
    • Translating: To guarantee that language is not a barrier to understanding, our Lingua volunteers translate our tales into hundreds of languages.
    • Protecting: Our Advox team fights for free expression online, focusing on legal, technical, and physical dangers to those who use the internet to express themselves in the public interest.
    • Empowering: Rising Voices gives training and mentoring to local marginalised communities that want to use participatory media techniques to share their own story.

    Except in the case of specially commissioned projects, TNC does not compensate writers or translators financially. TNC typically runs one or two active special projects at a time. Active projects include our Civic Media Observatory and our ongoing Translation Services project, which offers translation work to like-minded organizations for a fee.

    Community

    In addition to writing and translating stories, our community of volunteer contributors provides a breadth of local knowledge and connect TNC with like-minded initiatives around the world. Meet our contributors.

    Partnerships with the media and non-governmental organizations (NGOs) are also an important aspect of TNC' activity as a community, a media organisation, and a network of free speech activists. Learn more about our collaborations.

    Leadership and Governance

    TNC is led by a diverse, gender-balanced management, editorial and administrative team whose members come from many different countries and speak dozens of languages. Meet our leadership team.

    TNC is governed by a board of directors which include our founders, community representatives, and some of the most influential innovators in global online media. Board members serve without remuneration. Meet the board.

    Learn more about our work and team in our Roles and Responsibilities page of the TNC Community Guide.

    Legal Status and Funding

    Global Voices is incorporated in the Netherlands as Stichting Global Voices, a nonprofit foundation. We rely on independent grants, editorial commissions, and corporate and individual donations. Read more about our work and financial structure in our annual reports.
